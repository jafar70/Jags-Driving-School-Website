About Jag's Driving School

With the current economic climate we've noticed an avalanche of new Approved Driving Instructors (ADIs) & Potential Driving Instructors (PDIs) on the roads, all offering too- good- to- be- true deals. It's a minefield out there. How do you know which school is worth going with?

At Jags driving school I believe that you should enjoy learning to drive.

Once you have passed your test you will have gained a skill for life. I will work hard to make sure that your driving lessons are fun as well as informative because if you enjoy your lessons you will learn quicker.

I will take the time to discuss with you any previous driving experience you may have had and what your specific requirements are. I am particularly patient with my pupils and have helped many nervous people pass their test!
I am fully qualified, licensed and registered with the Driving Standards Agency (DSA), following strict rules and regulations laid down by them. I am also a (DSA) approved fleet trainer and provide Instructor training,
