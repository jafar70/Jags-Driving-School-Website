Fees & Appointments

Payments - Fees are payable, in advance, by way of cash at the first appointment, the fees for that and the next appointment are then payable. If you pay by cash, change is normally available, otherwise, you might be asked to carry over some of the balance to your next appointment. Jags driving school does not offer to take payment by credit or debit card because the card suppliers charge a fee for the facility, and this would have to be added to the lesson fee.

Cancellations - 24hrs notice is required for changes or cancellations, including public holidays, otherwise the full fee is payable unless your instructor, who takes a flexible approach, decides otherwise.

Non-attendance constitutes a late cancellation. Whilst using discretion, Jags driving school is not responsible for fees lost due to sickness, public transport delays or other causes of late cancellations. If the instructor cancels your appointments, you shall not be charged.
